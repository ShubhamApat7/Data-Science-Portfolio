BookMyShow Scraper

I had never made a scraper and the first scraper i was assigned to build was BookMyShow. I was asked to scrape the every event listed on all 1,898 cities in India across 4 categories (plays, sports, events, activities). BookMyShow is notoriously difficult to scrape. They have: Advanced bot detection Dynamic JavaScript content Rate limiting CAPTCHA challenges So as a newbie I started research on how to build a scraper from scratch, went on reddit, stack.com, medium and documentations of beautifulsoup, playwright and selenium.
